Our Services

Care

With our extensive range of staff members, we take the utmost care in making sure your pets are in tip-top condition by maintaining healthy lifestyles and ensuring regular exercise

Grooming

Our facilities contain all the tools and equipment to make sure your little furry pals are looking their best with a free grooming session given to every pet at the end of their stay

Walks

Along with our promise of regular exercise, we guarantee your pets will have multiple walks daily whether your furry pal is a small cat or a giant dog

Playtime

We provide extensive playgrounds, covering a total of 10km, for all different breeds, sizes and types of animals. It's a guarantee they wont be bored during their stay!

Tricks Training

For an additional cost, we are happy to train animals tricks such as sit, paw and roll over. We believe any animal is trainable with enough treats!

Bonding

With all the animals in our care, there will never be a shortage of friends for your pets. We encourage bonding between the animals throughout their stay
